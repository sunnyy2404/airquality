DATASET DETAILS

Dataset 1

Dataset 1 is provided by the Central Pollution Control Board (2020), the official portal of the Government of India.

The first dataset is a publicly available dataset collected in the course of six years (from January 2015 to June 2020).

The dataset contains -

1. samples - 29,531

2. columns - 18

3. Features - PM2.5, PM 10, Ozone (O3), Nitrogen Dioxide (NO2), NOx, NO, Sulphur Dioxide (SO2), Carbon Monoxide (CO), NH3, Benzene, Toluene, and Xylene

3. link - https://www.kaggle.com/rohanrao/air-quality-data-in-india

Dataset 2

The second public dataset is taken from Open Government Data (OGT) Platform India.

The dataset contains -

1. samples - 1574

2. columns - 12

3. Feautures - PM2.5 and PM10), Ozone (O3), Nitrogen Dioxide (NO2), Sulphur Dioxide (SO2), Carbon Monoxide (CO)

3. link - https://cpcb.nic.in/air-pollution/
